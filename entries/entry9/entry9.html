ORIOLE CHICAGO ❤️ NOVEMBER 22TH SUNDAY

Last night at Oriole Chicago was one for the books. My friends and I gathered to celebrate not just a meal but our friendship. From the moment we walked in, the atmosphere welcomed us warmly. The mix of laughter, soft jazz, and the clinking of glasses set the perfect tone for the evening.

The highlight of the evening? Sharing dishes that felt almost too beautiful to eat. Each course surprised us with flavors that were as comforting as they were creative. My personal favorite was the melt-in-your-mouth wagyu paired with a light truffle sauce, but honestly, everything was stunning.

What truly stood out wasn’t just the food—it was the laughter, the toasts, and the way the evening reminded us why we’re such good friends. As we walked out into the cool Chicago night, I couldn’t help but feel grateful for moments like this！
