WEDNESDAY 10PM
üìç53 W 53rd St, New York, NY 10019

This restaurant is located on 53rd street, next to MoMA, I made a reservation for Late night dining on Google Map a day in advance, and asked my friend to go to this restaurant with me after class at 9:40pm. It's worth mentioning that this restaurant is open until 2:00am, which is rare. The atmosphere in the restaurant was very nice, and the guests at the next table chatted with us and recommended the restaurant's delicious dishes. They were a couple from France, and they talked to us a lot about wine-tasting, which is an area I had never learned about before, and they made new friends.

It's an Asian fusion fine dining, and I felt that all of the dishes were creative and beautiful to look at, and each dish was like a work of art.
